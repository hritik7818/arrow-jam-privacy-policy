# arrowjam-privacy-policy

## Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** June 13, 2026

Thank you for playing our Arrow Jam game app.

This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the app.

### Information Collection and Use

This app does not collect, store, or share any personal information from users.

We do not require account creation, login, or personal details to use the app. Game progress and level states are stored locally on your device.

### Internet and Network Usage

The app may use internet access only for:

* App updates
* Google Play Services
* Crash reporting provided by platform services (if applicable)

No personal user data is intentionally collected or sold.

### Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party services provided by Google Play Services or Android system components that may collect limited technical information according to their own privacy policies.

Examples may include:

* Google Play Services
* Firebase Crash Reporting (if enabled)

Please review Google’s privacy policy for more information: [https://policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

### Children’s Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

### Data Security

Since we do not store personal user data, there is no user database or server-side storage associated with the app.

### Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated in the future. Any updates will be posted on this page.

### Contact

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, you may contact us through the Google Play Store developer contact information.

By using the app, you agree to this Privacy Policy.
